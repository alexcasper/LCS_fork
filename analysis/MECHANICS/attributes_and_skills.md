# Attributes and Skills

## Attributes

Every creature in Liberal Crime Squad has seven core attributes, each ranging from 0 to 99 (`MAXATTRIBUTE`).

| Attribute      | Description                                                        |
|----------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Strength**   | Physical power. Affects melee damage and grappling.                |
| **Intelligence** | Mental acuity. Governs technical and knowledge-based skill caps. |
| **Wisdom**     | Perception and judgment. Influences awareness-related skill caps.  |
| **Agility**    | Speed and coordination. Affects dodge, accuracy, and driving.      |
| **Health**     | Physical resilience. Reduces incoming damage and resists wounds.   |
| **Charisma**   | Social presence. Caps social and persuasion-based skills.          |
| **Heart**      | Ideological conviction. Unique to LCS — represents liberal passion and commitment to the cause. |

Attributes serve as **caps for associated skills**: a character cannot raise a skill beyond the value of its governing attribute.

## Skill Checks

Skill checks use a difficulty scale:

| Difficulty       | Target |
|------------------|--------|
| Very Easy        | 3      |
| Easy             | 5      |
| Challenging      | 7      |
| Hard             | 9      |
| Formidable       | 13     |
| Heroic           | 15     |
| Superheroic      | 17     |
| Impossible       | 19     |

A check rolls the character's skill value against the difficulty target. The margin of success or failure determines the outcome quality.

## Augmentations

Characters can undergo five types of augmentation, each enhancing a body region:

| Augmentation | Body Region | Effect                                   |
|--------------|-------------|------------------------------------------|
| Head         | Head        | Enhances intelligence or wisdom           |
| Body         | Torso       | Enhances health or strength               |
| Arms         | Arms        | Enhances strength or agility              |
| Legs         | Legs        | Enhances agility                          |
| Skin         | Skin        | Enhances health or charisma               |

Augmentations require the **Science** skill to perform and may have minimum age, cost, and difficulty requirements.
